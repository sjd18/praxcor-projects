Praxcor Projects

Advanced Oil & Gas Design Firm

Who We Are

Founded on the principles of precision and reliability, Praxcor Projects is a premier design firm dedicated to the energy sector. We specialize in transforming complex geological and infrastructural challenges into streamlined, robust engineering realities.

Our team consists of industry veterans, innovative structural engineers, and process design experts who understand the unique demands of upstream, midstream, and downstream operations.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, highly efficient, and sustainable design engineering that maximizes operational excellence while strictly adhering to rigorous regulatory and environmental standards.

We aim to protect our clients' assets, ensure workforce safety, and support the communities in which they operate through uncompromising design integrity and technological foresight.

Core Capabilities

Facility Engineering

Comprehensive layout and design for refineries, processing plants, and storage terminals. We ensure optimal footprint utilization and peak safety protocols.

Pipeline Networks

Advanced routing, stress analysis, and material selection for midstream transportation, ensuring longevity and leak-prevention under high-pressure scenarios.

Process Simulation

State-of-the-art computational modeling to optimize fluid dynamics, thermodynamic processes, and separation efficiency prior to physical construction.
