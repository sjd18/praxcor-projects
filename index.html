Who We Are

About Praxcor Projects

Founded on the principles of precision and reliability, Praxcor Projects is a leading design firm dedicated to the energy sector. We specialize in transforming complex geological and infrastructural challenges into streamlined, robust engineering realities. Our team consists of industry veterans, innovative structural engineers, and process design experts.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, highly efficient, and sustainable design engineering that maximizes operational excellence while strictly adhering to regulatory and environmental standards. We aim to protect our clients' assets and the communities in which they operate through uncompromising design integrity.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in oil and gas design globally, pioneering the transition towards smarter, safer, and technologically advanced energy infrastructure.

Our Capabilities

Facility Engineering

Comprehensive layout and design for refineries, processing plants, and storage terminals. We ensure optimal footprint utilization and peak safety protocols.

Pipeline Design

Advanced routing, stress analysis, and material selection for midstream transportation networks, ensuring longevity and leak-prevention under high-pressure scenarios.

Process Simulation

State-of-the-art computational modeling to optimize fluid dynamics, thermodynamic processes, and separation efficiency before ground is even broken.
